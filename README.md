## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of **Divyanshu Clothing Private Limited's sales performance in 2025** using **Microsoft Excel**.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help the company:

- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify sales trends and patterns.
- Evaluate product and channel performance.
- Make data-driven decisions to improve sales growth in 2026.

The analysis was conducted using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and an Interactive Dashboard**, resulting in actionable recommendations for business growth.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

> **To prepare the Annual Sales Report for 2025 so that Divyanshu Clothing Private Limited can better understand its customers and develop effective strategies to increase sales and profitability in 2026.**

---

# ❓ Business Questions

The company wanted answers to the following questions:

- Compare Sales and Orders throughout the year.
- Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?
- Who purchased more products: Men or Women?
- What were the different order statuses?
- Which states contributed the highest sales?
- What is the relationship between Age Group and Gender?
- Which sales channels generated the highest revenue?
- Which product category performed the best?
- What strategies can improve sales in 2026?

# 📈 Key Performance Insights

## 🏆 Peak Sales Month
- **March emerged as the best-performing month**, recording the highest sales revenue and the maximum number of orders.

---

## 👩 Customer Demographics
- Women contributed approximately **64% of total sales**.
- Female customers significantly outperformed male customers in purchasing activity.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Age Group Analysis
- The **Adult age group (30–49 years)** generated the highest number of orders.
- Adult women represented the company's most valuable customer segment.

---

## 🌍 Top 5 States by Sales

🥇 Maharashtra

🥈 Karnataka

🥉 Uttar Pradesh

🏅 Telangana

🏅 Tamil Nadu

These five states collectively contributed a significant portion of the company's total revenue.

---

## 🛒 Best Performing Sales Channels

🥇 Amazon

🥈 Myntra

🥉 Flipkart

The majority of the company's sales were generated through online marketplaces.

---

## 👕 Best Performing Product Category

### 🏆 Set Category

The **Set** category emerged as the highest-performing product category, generating the maximum revenue and recording the highest number of units sold.

---

## 📦 Order Status Analysis

| Order Status | Percentage |
|-------------|-------------|
| Delivered | 92% |
| Cancelled | 3% |
| Returned | 3% |
| Refunded | 2% |

The company maintained an excellent delivery success rate with over **92% successful deliveries**.

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

✅ Women are significantly more likely to purchase products than men.

✅ Adults aged **30–49 years** represent the company's largest customer segment.

✅ Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the primary revenue-generating markets.

✅ Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart are the most influential sales channels.

✅ The **Set** category is the company's strongest-performing product line.

✅ March recorded the highest customer demand and sales performance.

# 💡 Recommendations for 2026

## 🎯 Target High-Value Customers

Focus marketing efforts on:

- Women customers
- Age Group: **30–49 years**
- Customers residing in:
  - Maharashtra
  - Karnataka
  - Uttar Pradesh

---

## 📢 Strengthen Digital Marketing

Increase:

- Sponsored Advertisements
- Personalized Coupons
- Festival Campaigns
- Loyalty Programs
- Product Recommendations

on:

- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra

---

## 👕 Expand Best-Selling Categories

- Increase inventory of the **Set** category.
- Launch new designs and seasonal collections.
- Introduce bundle offers and discount campaigns.

---

## 📈 Prepare for Peak Demand

Since March generated the highest sales:

- Increase inventory before peak months.
- Plan promotional campaigns in advance.
- Improve supply chain preparedness.
